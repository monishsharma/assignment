PRODUCTS

For a sleek suit that exudes masculinity and elegance, Grado is a favourite suiting and shirting fabric brand for men across India. Available in classic, no-nonsense colours, the brand offers fabrics for every occasion.

The Grado Lineage


Grado as a brand has since evolved into one of the India’s best suit companies. Specialising in modern, innovative colours and shapes, Grado’s aesthetic is always on trend. With a wide range of fabrics offer to suit a variety of styles and silhouettes, this brand has a loyal following of clients in India.
